Pocket Audio Extractor

Extract audio from videos losslessly

Pocket Audio Extractor allows you to easily extract audio from your videos with no loss in audio quality. You can use this app on any Mac, iPad, or iPhone devices by purchasing only once.

2.99$

Features

Fast and easy to use

  • The simple user interface.
  • Instantly extract audio in Share Sheet from any app.
  • Processes files quickly with multi-threading.

Lossless extraction

  • Extract the existing audio track and place it into a new container, preserving the original audio quality without re-encoding.

Protect your privacy

  • All processing takes place on your device.
  • No Ad Tracking.
