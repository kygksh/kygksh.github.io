Privacy Policy

We do not provide personally identifiable information to third parties. We take great care in managing personal information and handle it as outlined below:

Our app includes a feature where you can send feedback, suggestions, and bug reports via email. To help us resolve issues, device information such as the OS version, mobile or tablet model, and app version may be sent.

Additionally, for the purpose of improving the app's usability, we conduct anonymous access analytics with great care, ensuring that individuals cannot be identified. For instance, in cases where the app crashes, we collect anonymous data about the specific area where the crash occurred, aiding us in promptly fixing bugs.

If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us.
